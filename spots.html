Vivid Cafe

✨ 4.6/5 – would sip again ✨

This place is ideal for working from a cafe, a date, meeting up with friends, and more. They roast their own beans and provide a ton of details on their source and attributes of the beans.

When all the other cafes are full, this one will still have space, not because it’s unpopular but because it’s really big. Whether you sit by the fireplace, the bar top stools at the window, or in the large back room, you’ll find the perfect spot.



Black Cap Coffee & Bakery

✨ 4.1/5 – nice vibes ✨

This cozy spot is perfect for a quick coffee run or a laid-back hangout. They offer a great selection of pastries alongside their brewed coffee. The ambiance is relaxed, with local art on the walls. I would go at a less peak hour of the day, because it is a popular spot!!

If you’re looking for a quiet corner or a seat by the window, you can always find your perfect spot here! Bring headphones and crank some work out-- P.S my fav drink there is the Iced chai with hazelnut and matcha xoxo
